Welcome to the Books Searcher


You can search for a book from a list of 5000 books by choosing "All Books" from the Navigation Bar or search by one of the following categories: ISBN, Title, Author or Year.

Once you have chosen the book, you will be able to see the complete data of its category and also, the assigned ratings and the reviews made by our users, as well as the score and number of ratings made by the users of the "goodreads.com" site.

In the "Api" section we will show you how to access the data in json format from your own api.

For all this you must enter as a user, which will also allow you to make a rating and a review of each book.
